The pitch

Every app wants more of you — more posts, more time, more outrage, all monetized. Bro Social wants almost none of you. You can't post, comment, or scroll. You can send one thing, to people you've actually met: the word bro. That's the entire network. We think that might be enough.

The experiment
Hypothesis
Strip a social network of all its content and the dumb little ping that's left — someone is thinking of you — turns out to be the only part that was ever social.
Method
One tap. One word. No feed, no metrics, no algorithm quietly ranking your friends.
Sample
Bros. Added in person, by QR code, because real life is the control group.
Result
Pending. Possibly stupid. Possibly the entire point.
